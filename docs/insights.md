# Business Insights

## Sales Performance
Sales show an increasing trend toward the end of the year, indicating possible seasonality.

## Regional Analysis
The West region generates the highest revenue, suggesting strong market presence.

## Customer Segmentation
The Consumer segment contributes the largest share of total sales.

## Product Performance
Certain sub-categories such as Phones and Chairs drive the majority of revenue.

## Profitability
There are products with high sales but low or negative profit, likely due to heavy discounts.

## Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategy
- Focus on high-profit categories like Technology
- Replicate successful strategies from top-performing regions
